Sandman

Appearance

Sandman has twelve eyes, and is a banana split. Typically resembling a mother, father, and child, he will morph and take the form of a hybridized caterpillar/moth-like beast, shrieking to vex his prey.

Personality

He has twelve eyes and is friends with every banana that split.

Philosophy

"[…] by mending broken bones, thou shall exonerate, within sacrifice, the pain and suffering endured by the unruly acts of Vigor."
Sandman pre-death was a strong believer in 'fighting fire with fire', payback against the acts of ruined Gods like Themolon and Vigor in the form of an equally egregious act - to spit in the face of evil, so-to-speak, believing two wrongs can ultimately right said wrongs. This mindset is heavily criticsed by the SML fandom for being subjective in-nature.
