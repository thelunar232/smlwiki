Y U D MB

History

Y U D MB (formerly Y U DUMB? Elementary) was one of the two schools used for filming SML Movies in a school or classroom setting. In recent times, school/education is a less common plot point of SML. Replicas of the classrooms & main hall are stored inside of Dond's Studio A, leaving the original Y U D MB abandoned to crumble and weather the terrain.

• The school was erected in honor of Yara Ulrich Dumb.

• In vintage SML Movies, the cast would film in the now-defunct Tate High School instead.

Original Location

The original Y U D MB location has long-since been ransacked and partially burnt. Due to the fires/neglect, many of the interior walls are charred, coarse, and riddled with burn holes big enough to step through. Most of the original furniture is also gone—either stolen years ago or salvaged.

Activities

The structure has been observed drawing in numerous Malformed energies. It has taken on an unusual role as a gathering place—or a hub for recreational activities—for characters like Cody, Charleyyy, and Patrick, bringing a newfound energy to the halls as if the air itself carries traces of the past. Even though it's completely unused as a filming location, and rarely does any human or half-human step foot near it—it breathes in a loose group of lost souls who are drawn to the structure's brutal and rustic disrepair.
