Silhouette

Description

'Silhouette' is a creature/entity of mysterious origin. It seems to have been human at some point, but is not anymore. It's known to stalk those who tread near the grounds of the old house. It makes a barely audible creaking sound wherever it goes.

Behavior

The entity tends to stay close to dark corners, or small spaces that have walls or other obstacles blocking any proper view of what's in front of or behind you at any given moment. For this reason, you're more likely to encounter it indoors, rather than outdoors. Those who have encountered this being have reportedly become 'cursed' in one way or another. The terrestrial limits of what this entity is capable of are undetermined.

Other Information

At this time, information on Silhouette is scarce and not easily obtainable. What is known is the fact that the entity is hostile and highly territorial. If you ever see its face, it would be in your best interest to escape into an open outdoor area with no walls. This is, however, known to not always work.

Some claim Lance got his powers through a connection to Silhouette, whenever the entity manifested, or later, through some spiritual/supernatural event, or even a curse. Though this makes logical sense, it is only speculation.
